Barcelona

Places worth visiting

La Sagrada Família is a famous basilica in Barcelona designed by Antoni Gaudí. Construction began in 1882 and is still ongoing. Known for its unique architecture, detailed facades, and colorful stained glass, it’s one of the city’s top attractions and a symbol of both faith and art.

Casa Batlló is a stunning modernist building in Barcelona, designed by Antoni Gaudí in 1904. It stands out for its colorful facade, organic shapes, and mosaic details inspired by nature. The roof resembles a dragon’s back, adding to its fairytale-like appearance.

Park Güell is a colorful public park in Barcelona designed by Antoni Gaudí. Built in the early 1900s, it features vibrant mosaics, winding pathways, and whimsical structures inspired by nature. Highlights include the famous lizard sculpture and the serpentine bench overlooking the city.
